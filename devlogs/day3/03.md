# Accessibility Cleanup

We reviewed the app with accessibility in mind and found several quick improvements that also make the interface clearer for everyone. The product is still early, but the foundations are improving.

Changes made:
- Strengthened contrast in the lesson and progress views.
- Added more descriptive labels around key interactive controls.
- Reduced reliance on color alone to communicate quiz correctness.

There is more to do here, but the app is noticeably easier to parse already.
