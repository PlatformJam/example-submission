# Content Model Decisions

We closed the day by defining how lessons and quizzes should be represented so content work can happen in parallel with frontend work. That should save us from hardcoding educational material into components.

Current direction:
- Lessons will be organized into sections with short prompts and optional graph actions.
- Quiz items will carry hints, explanations, and difficulty tags.
- Progress tracking will start simple with lesson completion and quiz score history.

Day one ended with more structure than code, which is the right tradeoff for this stage.
