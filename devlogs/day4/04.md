# Demo Storyline Drafted

We outlined the story we want to tell when showing LearnGraph to other people. That forced us to prioritize not just what the app can do, but what sequence best communicates its value.

Current demo arc:
- Start with a simple concept and show an interactive lesson.
- Move into a short quiz with contextual hints.
- End on progress tracking to show long-term learning potential.

This exercise clarified what still needs polish before the submission feels cohesive.
