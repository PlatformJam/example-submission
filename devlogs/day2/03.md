# First End-to-End Demo

We walked through the product from landing page to finished lesson and found the first real gaps. The good news is that the overall path works, and the weak spots are understandable rather than mysterious.

Observations from the walkthrough:
- Lesson transitions need clearer calls to action.
- The graph canvas needs stronger empty and loading states.
- Quiz completion should hand off directly into progress feedback.

This was the first moment the project felt like a product instead of disconnected experiments.
